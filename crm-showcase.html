1. Executive Summary

TravelOps is a comprehensive, cloud-native CRM and operations platform purpose-built for travel agencies. It replaces spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and manual ledgers with a unified digital workspace — from the first lead enquiry to post-trip feedback.

The Problem

Scattered Data
Leads in WhatsApp, invoices in Tally, itineraries in Word docs, payments in Excel
No Lead Tracking
No visibility into which leads are hot, cold, or lost — sales follows gut feeling
Manual Follow-ups
Missed callbacks, forgotten birthdays, no SLA enforcement
Revenue Blind Spots
No real-time P&L per trip, no vendor aging, no commission tracking

The Solution — TravelOps CRM

🎯
Lead Pipeline
Kanban board, auto-scoring, web capture, SLA timers, deduplication
📋
Quotes → Invoices
Versioned quotes, proforma, GST invoices, TCS compliance, PDF export
✈️
Itinerary Builder
Day-by-day planner, vendor pricing, dynamic costing, brochure PDF
🏨
Booking Manager
PNR tracking, vouchers, group bookings, cancellation policies
🤝
Vendor Ledger
Rate cards, TDS, multi-currency payments, 30/60/90 day aging
👤
Client 360°
Family profiles, visa tracking, RFM segmentation, travel history
💬
WhatsApp & Email
Bulk campaigns, templates, auto-reminders, birthday greetings
📊
Analytics
Revenue trends, booking funnel, staff KPIs, expense breakdown, P&L

Tech Stack

LayerTechnologyWhy
